The BBS

Remember when being online felt like going somewhere?

BBSes were places. You dialed in, said hello, played games, and got to know the regulars. The conversations were real because the people were real - and they stuck around.

Terminal Tavern is that kind of place, built on PhotonBBS - a modern BBS platform written from scratch. Not a museum piece, but a real system with the warmth of the ones we grew up on.

PhotonMUD

A world that builds itself.

PhotonMUD is a procedurally generated multi-user dungeon with D&D-style combat, character classes, spells, crafting, and a living economy. Every world is different.

Explore thousands of rooms, fight monsters, team up with other players, and carve your name into a world that grows with the community.

How it works.

Simple software, good values.

Easy to get started

Connect via telnet or SSH, create a free account, and you're in. No email required, no hoops to jump through.

Your business is yours

No tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Your conversations stay on the board. We're not interested in your data - we're interested in good company.

Runs on anything with a terminal

Telnet or SSH from your Mac, Linux box, Windows, or phone. We recommend PhotonTERM for the best experience.

Open source, always

PhotonBBS is GPL-licensed and on GitHub. Run your own board, modify the code, or submit a pull request. Terminal Tavern itself is community-driven - if you want a feature, you can help build it.

Active development

New games, new features, and regular updates. Built and maintained by someone who uses it every day.

Growing community

A friendly mix of old-school BBS veterans and curious newcomers. Come help us build something worth hanging around for.
